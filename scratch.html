About HERart

HERart is a passion project dedicated to recognizing the women who shaped art history. From the Renaissance to today, women artists have broken barriers, challenged norms, and created works of extraordinary beauty and meaning.

This page was built entirely from scratch using HTML and CSS — no Bootstrap, no templates. Just creativity and code.

Why Women in Art Matter

  1. Historical Impact
    • Women have created art for thousands of years
    • Many were excluded from formal art training
    • Their work was often attributed to male artists
  2. Breaking Barriers
    • Lavinia Fontana was the first woman to paint public commissions
    • Mary Cassatt was the only American in the French Impressionist group
    • Frida Kahlo turned personal pain into universal art
  3. Contemporary Voices
    • Carrie Mae Weems addresses race and gender through photography
    • Female artists today are redefining the art world
    • HERart exists to amplify these voices
