Our Mission

HERart is a digital platform dedicated to honoring the contributions of women artists — both historical and contemporary.
We exist to challenge gender biases in the art world and celebrate the resilience, creativity, and vision of women throughout history.

What We Offer

  • Curated profiles of historical women artists
  • A gallery showcasing contemporary female artists
  • An educational hub with articles and resources
  • A space for community engagement and discussion

Artists We Celebrate

From the Renaissance to today, HERart weaves a diverse and inclusive tapestry:

  1. Lavinia Fontana – Renaissance Pioneer
  2. Mary Cassatt – Impressionist Storyteller
  3. Frida Kahlo – Icon of Identity
  4. Carrie Mae Weems – Contemporary Voice

Inspired by the Guerrilla Girls

Taking inspiration from the Guerrilla Girls, HERart challenges norms and seeks to redefine the narrative surrounding women in art.
This is not just an observation of art — it is a reframing of history.
